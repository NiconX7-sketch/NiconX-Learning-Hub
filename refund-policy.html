Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

At NiconX Learning Hub, we strive to provide high-quality digital educational content. Due to the nature of our products (digital content that is accessible immediately upon payment), our refund policy is limited. Please read this policy carefully before making a payment.

1. Digital Content Policy

All content on NiconX Learning Hub (notes, practice questions, and revision materials) is digital and intangible. Once access is granted, the user immediately receives the full benefit of the service. Therefore:

  • Payments are generally non-refundable once access has been granted
  • We do not offer "change of mind" refunds
  • Free trial topics are available before payment to evaluate quality

2. Eligible Refund Situations

Refunds may be considered in the following exceptional circumstances:

  • Duplicate Payment: If you were charged twice for the same access session
  • Technical Failure: If you paid but cannot access content due to our system error (not user error)
  • Wrong Class Selection: If you accidentally paid for the wrong class and have NOT accessed any content (must contact us within 15 minutes)

3. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will NOT be issued for:

  • Change of mind after accessing content
  • User forgetting to use the access before expiry
  • User losing internet connection or device issues
  • User providing incorrect phone number for M-Pesa
  • Insufficient M-Pesa balance (the transaction will simply fail)
  • Content not meeting expectations (try free trial first!)
  • Parental or guardian disputes (all users must have permission)

4. How to Request a Refund

If you believe you qualify for a refund under Section 2 above:

  1. Contact us within 24 hours of the payment
  2. Provide your M-Pesa transaction receipt (SMS screenshot)
  3. Explain the reason for the refund request
  4. Include your registered phone number

Contact methods:

  • WhatsApp: 0110049657 (fastest response)
  • Phone/SMS: 0110049657 or 0101349001
  • Email: info@niconxlearning.co.ke

5. Refund Processing

If your refund request is approved:

  • Refunds are processed via M-Pesa reverse transaction
  • Processing time: 5-10 business days
  • You will receive an SMS confirmation from M-Pesa
  • Refunds go back to the same phone number that made the payment

6. Free Trial Recommendation

To avoid any risk of dissatisfaction:

  • Always start with the Free Trial before making a payment
  • The free trial gives you access to 2 complete topics per class
  • This allows you to verify content quality and relevance to your needs
  • If you like the free topics, then proceed to pay for full access

7. Chargebacks and Disputes

If you initiate a chargeback through your bank or M-Pesa without contacting us first:

  • Your access will be immediately revoked
  • Your phone number may be blocked from future payments
  • We reserve the right to dispute chargebacks with evidence of access granted

Please contact us FIRST to resolve any issues amicably.

8. Policy Updates

We reserve the right to update this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact for Refund Queries

For any refund-related questions:

  • Phone/WhatsApp: 0110049657 or 0101349001
  • Email: info@niconxlearning.co.ke
  • Response Time: 24-48 hours

Summary: Key Points to Remember

  • ✅ Try FREE trial before paying - 2 topics available!
  • ✅ Payments are generally non-refundable for digital content
  • ✅ Refunds only for duplicate payments or system errors
  • ✅ Contact us FIRST before disputing any charge
  • ✅ We're here to help - reach out on WhatsApp with any issues!
