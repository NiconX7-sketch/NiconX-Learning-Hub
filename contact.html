Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access content after payment?

After successful M-Pesa payment, you'll be automatically redirected to your dashboard where all topics for your selected class are unlocked.

What if I don't receive the STK Push?

Check if your phone number is correctly registered for M-Pesa. If the issue persists, contact us on WhatsApp with your phone number.

Can I get a refund?

Due to the digital nature of our content, we do not offer refunds once access has been granted. Contact us if you experience technical issues.

Do you offer content for higher grades?

Currently we offer Grades 4-6. Grades 7-8 and Form 1-4 are in development and will be added soon!
