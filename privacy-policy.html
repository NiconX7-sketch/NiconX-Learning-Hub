Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

NiconX Learning Hub ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Phone Number: Used for M-Pesa payments and access verification
  • Email Address: Optional - used for payment receipts and important updates
  • Payment Information: M-Pesa transaction receipts (stored securely)
  • Usage Data: Which topics you study, quiz answers, and time spent

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process M-Pesa payments via IntaSend payment gateway
  • To verify your access to paid content
  • To track your learning progress and suggest improvements
  • To improve our content and user experience
  • To communicate with you about your account and payment status
  • To prevent fraud and ensure security

3. Data Sharing

We share your information only with essential third-party services:

  • IntaSend: To process M-Pesa payments (they receive your phone number and payment amount)
  • Supabase: Our database provider (they store encrypted data securely)
  • Vercel: Our hosting provider (they store minimal technical data)

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted database storage (Supabase)
  • Secure API connections (HTTPS/TLS 1.2+)
  • Limited employee access to user data
  • Regular security reviews and updates

5. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for students with parental supervision. Parents/guardians are responsible for overseeing their children's use of our platform. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe your child has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us immediately.

6. Your Rights

Under Kenyan data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications
  • Lodge a complaint with the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC)

Contact us at 0110049657 or info@niconxlearning.co.ke to exercise these rights.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Payment records: 5 years (for financial compliance)
  • Usage data: 12 months (for service improvement)
  • User accounts: Until requested deletion

8. Cookies & Local Storage

We use localStorage (not cookies) to remember:

  • Your selected class (for convenience)
  • Your access status (to verify payment)
  • Your learning progress (to track completed topics)

This data stays on your device and is not sent to third parties. You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's local storage.

9. International Data Transfers

Our service providers (Supabase, Vercel, IntaSend) may store data on servers outside Kenya. We ensure all providers comply with applicable data protection laws and maintain appropriate safeguards.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy regularly.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Phone: 0110049657 or 0101349001
  • Email: info@niconxlearning.co.ke
  • WhatsApp: 0110049657
