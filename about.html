About NiconX Learning Hub

Empowering Kenyan students with affordable, quality revision materials

Our Mission

To democratize quality education by providing affordable, curriculum-aligned digital revision materials to every Kenyan learner regardless of their economic background.

Our Vision

To become Kenya's most trusted digital learning platform, empowering over 1 million students annually with accessible, high-quality revision materials.

Our Story

NiconX Learning Hub was born from a simple observation: many Kenyan students lack access to quality revision materials because textbooks are expensive and internet data costs are high. We created a solution that combines affordable pricing (20 KES), M-Pesa convenience, and mobile-friendly design.

The name "NiconX" combines "Nicodemus" (founder) and "X" representing excellence and the unknown potential in every learner. The "X" also honors Kate who gave the original idea.
